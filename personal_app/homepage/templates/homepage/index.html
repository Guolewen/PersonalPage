More About Me

My name is Lewen, Guo

I was born in Nanchang, where I had a fulfilling 18-year early life before I started my 4-year college life in Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Life in my undergraduate was kind of two-fold. On one hand, unfortunately I failed to enter into engineering schools to study any kind of "easy-to-get-a-job" major. However, on the other hand, I was able to invest more time on what I am interested in.
If summarized my 4-year college life in one sentence, I would say "technically no progress but mentally more matured". I endeavoured to pursue a "decent" job in China. However, it seems that employers in mainland are fond of computer geeks but not candidates with potentials.
I made a decision to move. Based on lots of reasons I decided to pursue a higher degree in Waseda University , Tokyo, Japan. During my master programme, I was lucky enough to have the opportunity to work as TA and RA in Waseda Business School and was writing Python and R programs for some research projects.
After my graduation, I was recommended by my master theis superviser Prof. Kawaguchi Yuichiro to join one of fast-growing listed real estate consulting firm - HyAS&Co. I was involved in developing a real estate service system for small real estate brokers.

Nanchang Foreign Language School

I studied at NFLS for six years (both middle and high schools), which is one of the best schools in Nanchang. At least, I laid relative good foundations in English language among my peers.

Waseda University

I got a Master of Science in Finance on 2018 Sept. at Waseda Business School. During my two-years studies, I worked for Prof. Kawaguchi Yuichiro and Prof. CHEUNG Ming Yan William as TA and RA. I wrote Python and R programs for several research projects and also involved in some writing parts using LaTeX

HyAs & CO.

After my graduation from Waseda University, I was recommended by my master thesis superviser to join one of the fast-growing real estate consulting firm HyAS&Co. As a member in R&D group, I was involved in developing real estate information system (REIDARS) I was responsible for developing both backend python scripts and front-end JavaScript scripts for some key functions and pages

Huazhong University of Science and Technology

College life in HUST is two-fold. To be honest, I didn't learn too much useful skills which may be advantegoues for me to find a decent job. Just as anyone in this world who has received higher education, 4-Year College life is something that evokes the inner philosophy of a man in his early 20's. The 4-year college teaches me, though not completely, but at least a sense of how this world works.
I participated in one of largest student association Aoikaze Anime Association where I made a lot of friends there. As a team we had arranged several ACG-related events which thousands of fans joined our events. unfortunately, two of my regrets when I was the president of this association are that we failed to hold our events in the famous venue -Optics Valley Gymnasium and we failed to issue our own Asscoiation Journal. I hope that my successors can fulfill these two tasks in near future.
